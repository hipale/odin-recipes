Oatmeal cookies recipe

    Ingredients
  1. Oats
  2. Peanut Butter
  3. Butter
  4. Eggs
  5. Sugar

    Directions
  • Preahet the oven to 175 degrees C
  • Mix sugar, butter and peanut butter together
  • To the mixture add some eggs
  • Add the oats and bake for 8 minutes
  • Enjoy the cookies!
